Delicous French Toast Recipe

The best french toast you'll ever have

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 4 slices of bread

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg, vanilla, and cinnamon in a shallow dish. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
  3. Cook on a greased skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of your choice.
