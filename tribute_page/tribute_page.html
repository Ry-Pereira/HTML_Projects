About Teezo Touchdown

Teezo Touchdown, born Aaron Lashane Thomas in Beaumont, Texas, is an American singer, songwriter, and producer known for his unique blend of rock, R&B, rap, and pop. With his signature nails-in-hair aesthetic and theatrical style, Teezo has built a reputation as one of the most creative and genre-defying artists of his generation.
                

He first gained attention through viral videos and creative music videos that showcased his bold personality and inventive visual style. Teezo has collaborated with major artists like Tyler, The Creator, Drake, and Travis Scott, and released his debut album “How Do You Sleep at Night?” in 2023 — a project praised for its emotional honesty and experimental sound.

More than just a musician, Teezo Touchdown is a true artist who blends fashion, performance, and storytelling into everything he creates
